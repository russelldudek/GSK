The pressure I see

The challenge is not finding AI ideas. It is earning the right to scale them.

The role points to a portfolio problem, a governance problem and a human adoption problem at the same time.

01

Portfolio pressure

Every function can generate a compelling AI use case. Scarce attention, sponsorship, Tech capacity and trusted data cannot support all of them at once.

How do we say yes to the few that matter most?
02

Bounded autonomy

Agentic systems can plan, call tools and act. Delegated authority must be explicit, observable and reversible.

Where may the agent act—and where must a human decide?
03

Pilot-to-scale gravity

A pilot can impress and still fail when data access, integration, controls, support, ownership and economics were never designed for scale.

Did we prove a model—or prove a product?
04

Behavior change

Utilization is not adoption. Sustainable value requires changed roles, habits, training, feedback and evidence that the workflow is better.

What would users miss if we turned the solution off?

Agentic AI product portfolio

Start with work that deserves intelligence, not work that happens to be automatable.

These are illustrative discovery lanes, not claims about GSK's internal roadmap.

Where are account teams rebuilding the same context before every consequential decision?

Explore governed agents that gather approved signals, prepare account context, surface changes, structure questions and maintain continuity across planning cycles—without inventing payer intent.

Inputs

Approved data, public policy, account history, evidence and contracting context.

Guardrails

Source traceability, no unsupported inference, human review and role-based access.

Measure

Preparation time, reuse, decision speed, user confidence and exception rate.

Which reimbursement and formulary workflows lose the most time to fragmented inputs and scenario rebuilding?

Use agentic orchestration to prepare structured scenario inputs, monitor approved information, route exceptions and support faster human analysis around coverage, reimbursement and access planning.

Inputs

Payer and formulary data, approved value materials, reimbursement rules and scenario assumptions.

Guardrails

Version control, approved sources, explicit assumptions, compliance review and audit trails.

Measure

Cycle time, scenario throughput, rework, assumption quality and decision confidence.

How much expert time is spent finding, comparing and reformatting evidence before judgment can begin?

Build controlled research workflows that retrieve, summarize, compare and trace approved sources while leaving interpretation, external claims and strategic decisions with accountable experts.

Inputs

Clinical evidence, HEOR/RWE materials, policy sources and approved internal knowledge.

Guardrails

Citation completeness, freshness checks, provenance, confidence markers and human approval.

Measure

Research cycle time, source completeness, reviewer edits, reuse and trust.

Which handoff becomes visible only after a deadline, stakeholder or launch outcome is already at risk?

Use agents to monitor workflow state, prepare handoffs, route approved tasks, surface dependencies and escalate exceptions—creating visibility without replacing accountable owners.

Inputs

Portfolio milestones, task state, dependencies, approved alerts and service commitments.

Guardrails

Authority matrix, escalation rules, reversible actions, observability and ownership.

Measure

On-time completion, handoff delay, escalation age, adoption and hours returned.

Governance by design

Agentic AI should explain not only what it did, but what it was allowed to do.

🧭

Decision rights

Define autonomy before launch. What may the agent recommend, prepare, route, execute or never do?

🔐

Data boundaries

Approved data is a product requirement. Role-based access, purpose limitation, provenance and privacy constraints.

🧾

Traceability

Every consequential action needs context. Inputs, tools, decisions, approvals, changes and exceptions should be observable.

🧑‍⚕️

Human accountability

Human-in-the-loop is not a checkbox. Review points must match actual expertise, authority and risk.

📈

Performance monitoring

Trust decays without measurement. Accuracy, drift, failures, utilization, latency and business outcomes require review.

🛑

Stop mechanisms

Scale is earned. Clear thresholds for pause, rollback, redesign and escalation protect patients and the business.
